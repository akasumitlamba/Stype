How it Works

01

Capture: Press your hotkey. Stype engages a low-latency audio stream via your system microphone.

02

Inference: OpenAI's Whisper model (int8 quantized) performs deep-learning transcription on your CPU.

03

Automation: Stype formats the text and simulates a system-level paste directly into your active cursor.
