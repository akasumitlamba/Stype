Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

At Stype, we believe your voice and your data belong exclusively to you. This policy outlines our strict, local-first approach to data handling.

1. Local Processing

Stype is a fully offline application. All audio captured via your microphone is processed locally on your computer using the Whisper AI model. No audio data is uploaded to the cloud, no text data is sent to external servers, and no internet connection is required for transcription.

2. Data Collection

Stype does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or telemetry.

3. System Permissions

To function, Stype requires temporary access to your microphone (for audio capture), keyboard hooks (to detect your global shortcut), and clipboard (to paste the resulting text).
