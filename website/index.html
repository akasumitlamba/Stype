Quickstart

Up and running
in sixty seconds.

Everything you need to go from download to your first dictated sentence.

01
Download & Install

Run the setup executable. Stype launches and parks itself in your Windows system tray. If SmartScreen prompts, click More Info → Run Anyway - expected for independent open-source apps.

02
Bind Your Hotkey

Right-click the tray icon, open Settings, and assign any global shortcut. Default is Ctrl + Space. Works across every app - browser, IDE, email, anywhere.

03
Speak & Paste

Press your hotkey once to begin, speak naturally, press again to finish. Stype shows a live status pill, then pastes the transcribed text directly at your cursor position.

Under the Hood

Engineered for silence,
speed, and precision.

Every decision in Stype is built to stay out of your way while you think.

Absolute Privacy

Zero audio leaves your machine. Whisper AI runs entirely on local hardware. No servers, no logs, no cloud calls.

Near-Zero Latency

faster-whisper with a CTranslate2 C++ backend. High-accuracy transcription on CPU - no GPU required.

Universal Injection

Works in every app - browsers, IDEs, Office. Suppresses OS hotkey conflicts and injects text at your cursor.

Smart Formatting

Automatic capitalization and punctuation. Text arrives clean and paste-ready - no cleanup needed.

Minimal Footprint

Low CPU at idle, modest memory, zero telemetry. Stype is present when you need it, invisible otherwise.

Real-Time Pill UI

A floating overlay cycles through Recording, Processing, and Pasted states. It vanishes when done - no clutter.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated - March 2026

At Stype, your voice and your data belong exclusively to you. This policy outlines our strict, local-first approach.

1. Local Processing

Stype is a fully offline application. All audio captured via your microphone is processed locally using the Whisper AI model. No audio is uploaded, no text is transmitted externally, and no internet connection is required for transcription.

2. Data Collection

Stype does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. There are no cookies, tracking pixels, crash reporters, or telemetry of any kind.

3. System Permissions

Stype requires temporary microphone access for audio capture, keyboard hooks to detect your global shortcut, and clipboard access to paste the resulting text. These permissions are used solely for their stated purpose.
