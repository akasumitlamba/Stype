# Privacy Policy for Stype

**Last Updated: March 22, 2026**

At Stype, we believe that your voice and your data belong to you. This Privacy Policy explains how Stype handles information.

### 1. Offline Processing (Local First)
Stype is designed to be a fully offline application. All audio captured via the microphone is processed locally on your computer using the `faster-whisper` AI model. 
- **No audio data** is ever uploaded to the cloud.
- **No text data** is ever sent to external servers.
- **No internet connection** is required for the core functionality of the app.

### 2. Data Collection
Stype **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of telemetry to monitor your usage.

### 3. Third-Party Access
Because the app runs entirely on your local hardware, no third parties have access to your recordings or the text generated by the app.

### 4. System Permissions
Stype requires the following permissions to function:
- **Microphone Access:** To capture your voice for transcription.
- **Keyboard Hooks:** To listen for your custom global hotkey.
- **Clipboard Access:** To paste the transcribed text into your active application.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
If we update Stype to include features that require internet connectivity, this policy will be updated accordingly.

### 6. Contact
If you have any questions regarding your privacy while using Stype, please contact the developer via GitHub: [https://github.com/akasumitlamba/](https://github.com/akasumitlamba/)
